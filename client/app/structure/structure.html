Spark Structure

Spark is a three-tier mega event, with the first phase being the SPARK Junior Quiz, a pan-India quiz competetion to be conducted in over 20 cities, far and wid across the nation. With 4000+ participants, the Spark Junior Quiz is indeed one Epic Quizzing Journey. The winners stand a chance to travel all the way to IIT Madras free of cost to compete in the national finals and experience what Shaastra has to offer.

A brief structure of SPARK is given below:

Spark Prelims

  • General Tech Quiz-science facts, logic, tech and how things work
  • Written Round
  • 30 questions-one word answer
  • Team of 2
  • Top 2 teams get prizes
  • Top 5 teams from each city qualify for Nationals Screening Round

    • Spark Screening Round

  • Questionnaire-1 Page report on given topic(case study competition)
  • Online Submossion
  • Participation will be guided by the Shaatra team
  • Top 25 go for the National finals- the Spark Junior Conference

    • Spark Finals

  • Presentation-based on the report submitted for screening round
  • Keynote and Lectures
  • Workshops & Events
