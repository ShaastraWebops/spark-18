General Instructions

  1. Students should come in teams of two.
  2. There are no limitations on the number of teams participating from a particular school.
  3. The team members should be from the same shool/branch. No cross teams allowed.
  4. On-spot registrations are only available subject to availability of seats.
  5. Please be present early as there are limited seats.
  6. Please carry a pen, a writing pad and your school identity card.
  7. 10th passouts who have just been admitted to 11th may bring their provisional admission receipt or previous school ID card.
  8. Further details regarding will be displayed on this page.

(If your school doesn't provide an ID card, bring the school diary/ calendar, or in case there is no identity proof from school please bring any other ID proof such as Aadhar Card.)

Format

  • The quiz will consist of only one round - A common written round.The winners will be decided based on the marks scored.
  • Prizes and Incentives
    • 1st to 3rd : Medal + Certificate + Qualification for the Nationals Screening round.
    • 4th and 5th : Qualification for the Nationals Screening round.
  • The organisers's decision on any matter pertaining to the quiz would be final and binding. The organisers can be amend the eligibility and rules of participation as deemed it.

    • Syllabus

    General Knowledge questions based on the topics of science, maths and technology.
